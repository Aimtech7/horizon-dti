Our Programs

German Language Programs Designed for Real-World Success

Learn German. Live German. Succeed Globally.

Germany is urgently seeking skilled professionals in healthcare, education, and hospitality due to a nationwide labour shortage. With generous opportunities on offer, now is the perfect time to step in. The key to unlocking these life-changing roles? Fluency in German, your gateway to a secure and successful future abroad.

A Modern Language Gateway for Global Achievers

At Horizon Deutsch Training Institute, our programs are carefully structured to help learners progress confidently, communicate effectively, and unlock opportunities in Germany and German-speaking countries. Whether you are starting from zero or advancing toward professional fluency, our programs combine language mastery, cultural understanding, and career readiness.

Course Levels

German Language Levels (A1–B2)

Choose the right level for your current proficiency and goals.

A1 – Beginner Level

Foundation Building

Course Duration

8-12 weeks (part-time) or 4-6 weeks (intensive)

Class Schedule

Morning, Evening, or Weekend options available

What You'll Learn

Basic German grammar, essential vocabulary, simple conversations, introductions, and everyday expressions. Focus on speaking, listening, reading, and writing fundamentals.

Learning Outcomes

Introduce yourself and others, ask and answer simple questions, understand and use familiar everyday expressions, and interact in a simple way.

Enroll in A1

A2 – Elementary Level

Building on Basics

Course Duration

8-12 weeks (part-time) or 4-6 weeks (intensive)

Prerequisites

Completion of A1 level or equivalent proficiency

What You'll Learn

Expanded vocabulary, more complex grammar structures, past and future tenses, and enhanced conversational skills for various situations.

Learning Outcomes

Communicate in simple, routine tasks, describe aspects of your background, immediate environment, and matters in areas of immediate need.

Enroll in A2

B1 – Intermediate Level

Independent Communication

Course Duration

12-16 weeks (part-time) or 6-8 weeks (intensive)

Prerequisites

Completion of A2 level or equivalent proficiency

What You'll Learn

Advanced grammar, professional vocabulary, complex sentence structures, and cultural nuances. Focus on workplace and academic German.

Learning Outcomes

Understand main ideas of complex text, interact with a degree of fluency, produce clear, detailed text on various subjects, and explain viewpoints.

Enroll in B1

B2 – Upper Intermediate

Professional Proficiency

Course Duration

12-16 weeks (part-time) or 6-8 weeks (intensive)

Prerequisites

Completion of B1 level or equivalent proficiency

What You'll Learn

Near-native fluency, specialized vocabulary for professional fields, academic writing skills, and advanced cultural understanding.

Learning Outcomes

Understand complex texts, express yourself fluently and spontaneously, use language flexibly for social, academic, and professional purposes.

Enroll in B2
