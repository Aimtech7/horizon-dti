About Us

We believe learning German isn’t just about passing an exam, it’s about transforming your future.

Who We Are

Join Us – Your Future Speaks German

Horizon Deutsch Training Institute is a dedicated German language training institution in Kenya, committed to preparing learners for academic, professional, and cultural integration into Germany and other German-speaking countries.
Founded on the belief that language is the foundation of global opportunity, we provide structured German language programs that go beyond grammar — equipping students with practical communication skills, cultural awareness, and career-oriented readiness.

Our Vision

To become a leading centre of excellence in German language education and international pathway preparation, empowering learners to confidently access global opportunities.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, student-centred German language training that enables learners to:

✔ Communicate confidently in real-life and professional settings

✔ Integrate successfully into German society and workplaces

✔ Access education, employment, and vocational opportunities abroad

We achieve this through structured learning, experienced instruction, and a supportive academic environment.

Our Teaching Philosophy

At Horizon Deutsch Training Institute, we believe that learning German should be practical, immersive, and purpose-driven.

Our approach emphasises:

✔ Speaking and listening from day one

✔ Context-based learning tied to real situations

✔ Continuous assessment and guided progression Cultural understanding as an essential part of language mastery

We prepare our students not just to pass exams, but to use German confidently in daily life, work, and study environments.

Common Questions

Do you support Ausbildung applications and placement?

Yes. We assist with language preparation, document review, application guidance, and interview coaching for Ausbildung institutions and employer partners.

I’m a Form Four leaver — can I apply?

Yes. Form Four graduates can enroll in our School Leavers Program, which offers German training, career guidance, and pathways into vocational training and study opportunities abroad.

What is the Au Pair program and who can apply?

The Au Pair program allows young women to live with a host family in Germany while learning German and gaining cultural experience. We guide applicants through language training, documentation, and application support.

Do you offer group or polytechnic discounts?

Yes. We provide discounted rates for group enrollments and students from partner polytechnics.

Are flexible payment options available?

Yes. We offer student-friendly installment plans to make our programs accessible.

What documents are required to enroll?

To enroll, you only need a valid ID or passport and basic contact details. Additional documents may be required for international programs.

How do I apply?

You can apply online through our website, contact us via phone or WhatsApp, or visit our office or partner institutions for guided assistance.

Where can I find official information about Germany?

You may explore trusted sources such as:

  • Make it in Germany – Germany’s official government portal

  • DAAD – Study opportunities in Germany

  • Germany’s Federal Employment Agency

Where can I find official information about German exams in Kenya?

Official exam details are available through recognised providers such as Goethe-Institut Kenya, ÖSD, and TELC. Our team will guide you on exam selection, preparation, and registration.

What is the Kenya–Germany Labour Migration Agreement?

The Kenya–Germany Comprehensive Migration and Mobility Partnership is a formal agreement that creates legal and structured pathways for Kenyan professionals to access employment and training opportunities in Germany, especially in healthcare, technical trades, and services. It supports language training, vocational preparation, and fair labour mobility.

Do I need prior knowledge of German to enroll?

No. Our A1 program is designed for complete beginners. You can start learning German with no prior experience.

How long does it take to complete A1–B2 levels?

Most learners complete A1 to B2 within 6–9 months, depending on attendance, practice, and individual learning pace.

Do you offer online German classes?

Yes. We offer live virtual German classes as well as in-person lessons at our campus and partner institutions, allowing learners to study from anywhere.

What class schedules are available?

We offer morning, afternoon, and evening classes, making it convenient for students, working professionals, and school leavers.

Can I switch between online and in-person classes?

Yes. Subject to availability, students may switch between virtual and physical classes with guidance from our admissions team.

Which German exams do you prepare students for?

We prepare learners for internationally recognised German exams including Goethe-Institut, ÖSD, and TELC.

Will I receive a certificate after completing a level?

Yes. Upon completing each level, students receive a course completion certificate and guidance on registering for official international exams.

Do you provide exam preparation support?

Yes. Our exam preparation includes mock tests, exam techniques, structured revision, and registration guidance to help students succeed with confidence.

Do you assist with study or work opportunities in Germany and Austria?

Yes. Through our education, vocational, and employer partners, we support pathways into Ausbildung, Au Pair programs, vocational training, and employment opportunities.

What is Ausbildung and how does it benefit my career?

Ausbildung is a paid vocational training program in Germany and Austria that combines classroom learning with workplace experience, leading to internationally recognised qualifications and strong employment prospects.
